// how-this-works

Speaker-first, not talk-first.

The sessions page lists talks. This page flips it: one card per speaker, so you can see the whole arc of what someone's brought to the chapter — and follow their socials to catch what they do next. Future speakers get their own profile the moment their first talk ships.
