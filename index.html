// chapter / what-we-stand-for

A community of cloud security practitioners in Pakistan. Independent. Run by engineers.

CNSPK is its own thing. We are engineers, students, and security leads in Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad — meeting online and in person, and building a body of practice that didn't exist here before.

Affiliated with CNCF as a recognised security chapter. Not a CNCF franchise.

PILLAR 01

Rigor

What is true matters more than what sounds good. We test, we cite, we date-stamp our corrections.

PILLAR 02

Belonging

The first question is the most valuable one in the room. No gatekeeping on credentials, English fluency, or city.

PILLAR 03

Bridge

We connect Pakistani engineers to KubeCon, OSS contribution, and the global CNCF in both directions.
